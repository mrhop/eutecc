HD CEFC Promotion Event in Nuremberg

On 16th May 2017, a successful HD CEFC promotion event was held in Nuremberg, Germany. The event was in the historic rooms of the Tucher Castle and was fully booked with 70 companies which are all EU technology leaders!

The guest speakers included Mr. Dr. Fraas, the Vice Mayor of Nuremberg as well the international head of HVB, one of Germany leading banks. During the event Dr. V.Tucher, Mr. Conrad and Mr. Florian Schmied, who is the chairman of Tucher Group, introduced the HD CEFC project and the Chinese market opportunities to the guests.
