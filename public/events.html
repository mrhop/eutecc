EVENTS SUPPORTED BY EUTECC

  • Intelligent Production in Guangdong vs Industry 4.0 in Germany

    On Apr 8th 2016, the Intelligent Production in Guangdong vs Industry 4.0 in Germany was held by Shenzhen Longgang District People’s Government in Munich. On the conference, a “Strategic Cooperation Framework on the Pilot Project of China EU Future City” was signed in between the following 5 parties: Shenzhen Longgang District People’s Government, Tucher Group, EUTECC, TUM Int’l GmbH, and Medical Valley EMN.

  • China-Germany Symposium on Economic and Trade Cooperation

    On Sep 12th 2016, China-Germany Symposium on Economic and Trade Cooperation was held in Munich, coorganized by Shenzhen Municipal People‘s Government and the State Government of Bavaria Germany. Many European companies entered into cooperative agreement with us on our projects in China. Mr Florian Schmied, Chairman of Tucher Group and EUTECC, gave a report on the Economic Development in Shenzhen, Cooperation with Germany and Industry 4.0 in China and Germany.

  • 1st China EU Future City Medical Cluster Conference-Senior Care Facility Launch Ceremony

    China EU Future City, supported by EUTECC, successfully brought in the largest senior care group in Germany, and the Senior Care Facility Launch Ceremony was held in Shenzhen Longgang District On Nov 21st 2016. Dr. Florian Janik , Mayor of Erlangen in Germany, witnessed the ceremony.

  • 2017 HD CEFC Promotion Event-Munich

    On Mar 24th, supported and co-organized by EUTECC, Huande China EU Future City Promotion event was held in Bayerischer Hof Hotel in Munich Bavaria. Mr Scharping, Former Minister of Defense in Germany and European Socialist Parties, and Dr Wagner, Head of International Business of HVB, attended the event. Over 50 European companies took part in it.

  • 2017 HD CEFC Promotion Event-Nuremberg

    On May 16th 2017, HD CEFC Promotion Event was held in Tucher Castle in Nuremberg. Dr Fraas, the Vice Mayor of Nuremberg, and Dr Wagner, Head of International Business of HVB, addressed the event. More than 50 European compa- nies participated in the event, many of which signed cooper- ation agreement with us on the spot.

  • 2017 HD CEFC Promotion Event-Zurich

    On July 6th 2017, the HD CEFC promotion event was success- fully held in Zurich, the biggest city in Switzerland. Mr. Wybren Tearpestra from GIC, one of the largest specialized steel man- ufacturers and ship builders in the world, Mr. Yusuf Savmaz, Head of Zurich Region, Credit Suisse AG, and more than 50 leading high-tech companies from Europe actively participa- ted in the event.
